Quantum Gravity

Quantum Gravity is a field of Quantum Physics that attempts to explain gravity according to quantum mechanics, such as in black holes or astrophysical singularities. In general relativity, mass & energy are all given defined values. These are represented with tensor points associated with an individual spacetime point. These quantities then determine a function of a spacetime metric. However, the theories describing our 4 fundamental forces (gravity, electromagnetic, strong nuclear force, & weak nuclear force) are quantum theories, meaning they have variable values. This is an expression of Heisenberg's Uncertainty Principle.
